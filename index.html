JSON Parsing App

This tool will accept geographic data in JSON format, parse though the information and display your choice of park from the drop down menu on the OpenLayers Webmapping Service. The map will zoom to the park automatically.

Highlight and copy the JSON text from the box below, then paste it into the box below and click on "Load the List" button
